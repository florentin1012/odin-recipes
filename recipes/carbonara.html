Spaghetti carbonara

Italian delight

Description

Spaghetti carbonara is a type of pasta dish very often misunderstood by non-italians.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • Grana padano
  • Guangiale
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Whisk beaten egg with grated grana padano and your seasonings in a bowl.
  2. Boil your pasta to al dente.
  3. Fry your Guangiale in a bit of olive oil
  4. Add your egg to the hot skillet.
  5. Quickly finish your pasta in the sauce.
